1. Project BackgroundThis project serves as a comprehensive web design course presentation for a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program. It is conceptualized as a strategic and visual overhaul of the Starbucks digital ecosystem.2. The Business ProblemCurrently, ordering coffee online feels transactional and utilitarian. The challenge is to maintain the functional reliability of the original Starbucks website while completely reinventing the user experience to be modern, fun, and visually arresting.3. The Strategic Solution
By introducing features like AI mood ordering, gamified reward systems, and hyper-fast commuter modes, the platform transforms into a sticky, habit-forming digital product that drives higher engagement and increased upselling opportunities through highly visual, interactive customization.
