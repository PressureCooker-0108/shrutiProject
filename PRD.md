1. Product Vision
The goal is to transition Starbucks from a static, corporate utility into an engaging, fluid, and personalized digital destination. We are moving away from standard utility interfaces toward an emotional, hyper-personalized web experience.

+ 1

2. Target Audience

The Daily Commuter: Needs frictionless, ultra-fast ordering.

The Gen-Z/Millennial Enthusiast: Values aesthetics, gamification, and highly customized aesthetic drinks.

The Undecided Customer: Experiences "menu fatigue" and needs curated, mood-based suggestions.

3. Core Features

"Liquid" 3D Drink Builder: A visual, interactive builder where a 3D or high-quality 2D vector glass dynamically updates as the user selects ingredients. The liquid changes color, ice cubes drop in with a slight bounce, and cold foam cascades over the top.  
MD
+ 1

"Brew Your Mood" AI Ordering: A horizontal, swipeable series of dynamic color orbs that recommend curated drinks based on moods like "Tired," "Cozy," or "Need to Focus".  
MD

Commuter Mode: A hyper-fast feature that transforms the hero section into a "One-Tap Reorder" screen showing a live estimated queue time for the local store.  
MD

Gamified "Constellation" Rewards: An interactive night sky canvas where users connect earned "Stars" to unlock different tiers of rewards, adding a game-like dopamine hit.  
MD

4. Success Metrics (KPIs)

Decrease in average time-to-checkout for returning users (via Commuter Mode).

Increase in average order value (AOV) via visual upselling in the 3D Drink Builder.

Increased engagement time on the Rewards page.
